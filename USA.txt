The United States is the world's third-largest country by land and total area behind Russia and Canada.[c][156][157] The 48 contiguous states and the District of Columbia occupy a combined area of 3,119,885 square miles (8,080,470 km2).[158][159] The coastal plain of the Atlantic seaboard gives way to inland forests and rolling hills in the Piedmont plateau region.[160]
